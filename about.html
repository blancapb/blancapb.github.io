Hi, I'm Blanca!

I’m a committed and creative communicator with experience in social media, marketing and community management.

In recent years, I have worked with social media and content for student-run events and campaigns, as well as being responsible for marketing for a game project. It has given me practical experience in planning, creating and implementing projects where many pieces have to fall into place. Outside of work, you can often find me at a boba spot, behind a camera, or at some gaming industry event in Stockholm!

Juggling several projects at the same time is not a problem for me, on the contrary, I enjoy many balls in the air best! With experience from both non-profit projects and the gaming industry, I've learned a lot about structure and problem solving, I'm quick to find ways forward when something doesn't go as planned. Organizing and keeping track of schedules and deadlines comes naturally to me (Trello and Notion are my best friends🙌)

Gaming culture is close to me so I know how to speak the language of the target group, and in everything I do I try to carry optimism with me! I believe in meeting both people and challenges with a positive attitude. If you are looking for someone who is committed and ready to contribute energy and new ideas: please get in touch!
